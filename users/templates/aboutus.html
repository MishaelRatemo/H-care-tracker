About Us

How we Began

The idea to start H.Care Tracker started from the need of medicine in our villages. We needed medicine urgently in our local clinic and after many days of waiting the supplies that had been ordered had not arrived as scheduled.

This brought about the urgent need to find a system that would allow us to observe the supplies wehad ordered and to be able to track them until they got to their intended destination.

What we Do

We are a tracking company that provides the service of tracking your supplies from supplier to reciever and to ensure that what has been ordered is exactly what arrives

Vision

To get all affordable and equitable supplies to citizens throught all rural areas and settlements

Mission

To build a reputable distribution system that will serve all equally and ensure accountable, equitable distribution of medical supplies.

Our H.Q

Our offices are currently at Moringa. This is where we run our operations from and for the foreseable future will be held here for ease of access to our generous and wonderful clients
